Progressive dissolution of the British Empire

EN

The British Empire was the greatest Empire ever created. It had both the most population and area in the History of all Empires. Even though it was the strongest after India and Pakistan got their independence all of the other colonies soon followed. They started rebeling against the British Empire in hopes to get their independence as well. This lead to numerous rebellions and battles for freedom. Britain couldn't keep up with the rebellions and one by one all colonies got their independence leading to the Dissolution of The British Empire
